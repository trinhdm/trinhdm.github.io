About

Foreground Studios, a subset of Foreground Clothing, is an up and coming media team that specializes in photography and videography. They have been featured in City Gala at the Playboy Mansion, among other events.

Approach

Initially, I was brought onto the team as a marketing intern, where I helped out in creating and editing content for their Kickstarter. As the company migrated their goals towards forming a media team, I was given the opportunity to try my hand in Wordpress and Shopify development, as my mentor quickly realized my development skills exceeded their exceptions.

As their sole web developer, I utilized Photoshop, Illustrator and Invision to wireframe and mock-up the website before proceeding with development. I then utilized HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and PHP to craft a site that fit their needs.

This project has proved to be invaluable for understanding the complexity of developing a Wordpress Theme. It gave me great insight on how Wordpress worked in the front-end and back-end, and it also taught my how to work with clients. Through creating this theme, I have also taught myself PHP.
