Bound is a mobile platform for busy story lovers. They deliver stories in an easy-to-engage format that combines prose fiction, stunning visuals, and dramatic audio. Early access is coming soon.

Overview

Currently, the Bound site is under construction -- the screenshots will be updated as the project goes on.

The founders of Bound, Matthew and Jen Hannus, brought me onto the team as the sole front-end developer and designer. Within this project, I was in charge of designing and developing a reusable WordPress theme, whose purpose was to showcase and preview each story series that is within the upcoming app.

Showcasing Each Series

We wanted to entice the user into downloading the app as soon as they scrolled through the home page, so we opted for a slider that could incorporate both videos and pictures. We thought that it would be a nice touch if each slide fades onto one another as the user scrolls down the page, as opposed to a tradition slider. The slider then fades into an excerpt of the series, which then leads to an email sign up form to sign up for the app. I wrote a walk-through on how I created the slider here.
