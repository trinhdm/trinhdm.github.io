• Chinese Association @ UCI

    About

    Chinese Association at UC Irvine is a social, cultural, and community service-based club located at the University of California, Irvine. Their mission is to share Chinese and Chinese-American culture with the greater Orange County and Los Angeles area.

    Approach

    For the new Chinese Association website, my role was to wire-frame and design a website, and then convert those designs into a responsive WordPress theme. I worked closely with the board members of Chinese Association to implement a design that fit their needs and their members' needs. This website was developed with HTML5, CSS3, jQuery and PHP.
