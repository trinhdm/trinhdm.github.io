Hello! We are Bound.

Stories live here - Hand-crafted stories built for mobile.

Bound is a mobile platform for busy story lovers. We deliver stories in an easy-to-engage format that combines prose fiction, stunning visuals, and dramatic audio. All of our series are professionally produced by leading authors, artists, and creatives and then brought to you via short sessions, in-depth companion material, and integrated community features.

A Sneak Peek

Bound is an experiment in storytelling. We are trying to build interesting worlds for people to explore, one story at a time. We plan to open early access in May 2016, but the video below shows a little sneak-peak.
