Chinese Association's 30th Annual Culture Night

May 14th, 2016 | Bren Events Center

Culture Night is our annual student-run production and our largest event of the year! We feature over 100 participating members and students in a variety of art forms, including a cultural skit, traditional and contemporary dance, martial arts, and guest performances. Each year we are proud to showcase Chinese culture to over 500 members of the UCI community.

For sponsorship inquiries, please contact president@cauci.com.

戲 | Cast

Our cast of talented actors and actresses bring passion to the stage! Each year, Culture Night cast performs an original play based on China's traditions, culture, and history.

舞 | Traditional Dance

Our traditional dance teams bring grace and power to the stage! Culture Night features all-girls, all-guys, and co-ed dance teams coordinated by student dancers.
