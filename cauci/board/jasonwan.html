Why did you join CA? I wanted to get involved on campus. I realized I wasn't getting the "full college experience" by sitting in my dorm and playing League of Legends until daybreak. I joined Culture Night Traditional Dance and made lasting friendships with the people I met there.

Why did you join board? After being a part of Traditional Dance, I fell in love with the magic of Culture Night. I decided I wanted to reproduce that magic for others to enjoy. So I ran and was appointed Cultural Chair. I then returned for 2 more terms and that's how I got here as President.

What are your hobbies? I enjoy art, tv shows, anime, and food.

Favorite music genre and artists? I like electronic music that has good vocals. In terms of artists, I like Passion Pit, but my favorite is Clean Bandit due to their being classically trained and incorporating string instruments into their music.

What's your favorite CA event? Capture the flag! It's a fun chance to get out there and play some sports with everyone. I do tend to get a little competitive, but it's all for fun!

What's your favorite memory from Ski Trip? The drive up is definitely the most memorable. Not saying anything more about it haha. Whatever happens at Ski Trip, stays at Ski Trip.

What's your favorite annual event and why? Culture Night! It'll always have a special place in my heart.

What's your favorite part of Culture Night? Cultural Dance! It's a fun way for people to get connected with Chinese culture and learn something new. I might do dance this year.

What's your favorite pun?? What do you call a stuck-up thief walking down a flight of stairs? A condescending con descending.
