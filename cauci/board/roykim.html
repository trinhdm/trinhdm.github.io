Why did you join CA? I joined CA mainly because I didn't like the fact that I was spending my entire freshman year lounging around in the dorms. In high school, I grew from a shy and reserved person to becoming very involved and outgoing. Coming into college, I wanted to explore a new social life outside of my hall, and CA was the perfect opportunity to meet new people as well as escape school.

Why did you join board? I joined board because I wanted to experience CA in a different way than I had as a member for two years. I was impressed by the student leadership and commitment in the past years, and just like how I knew I had a lot to offer to the club, CA had a lot to offer for myself. Becoming part of the board team meant I would develop my leadership and time management skills, but also create memories that would carry on to the next generations to come. Lastly, I also joined CA to meet new faces, especially alumni and those from other UC schools.

What's your favorite annual event and why? I think my favorite annual event (AND MOST IMPORTANT) is the Family Revealing Dinner.
