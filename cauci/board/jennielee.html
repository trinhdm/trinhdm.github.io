First Year in CA 2013

Why did you join CA? I went to orientation because of my friend Evasia. And then I saw how wacky people were and I thought, "hey, I can kinda see myself with those people." And then after orientation Kenny Doan convinced me to go to Car Rally with his backflips. And then I thought I'd try to go to every single week if I could and then the rest was history.

Why did you join board? So many reasons. But it all boiled down to "I will regret my life if I didn't join CA board." Fun times, friends, service, culture, and self-growth; what's not to like?

What are your hobbies? Art, writing, video games. Reading, swimming, tennis. Palm reading, massaging, archery. Legos? I'm just listing off the top of my head...

Favorite music genre and artists? ummm music that kinda goes in a wave. And something that I can bob to or sing. Did that make sense?

What's your favorite CA event? Slenderman because it was hilariously exciting and fun, much more than I expected. I got to dress up again (yay!) and scare the bejeebus out of people. Everyone got a good amount of exercise too~ And I came up with the concept so it's my baby.

What's your favorite memory from Ski Trip? Going to Lake Tahoe in Van 3 with the then board officers Caroline and Jason was cool. Snowboarding. Midori Sour! That was the best thing evarrr. I was also giving massages to people to live vicariously through them because I was sore AF myself. Also Vanessa Leung trying to "massage" people was hilarious (pat pat arm). Also cabbages. Making adorbs snow sculptures with my friends Ray, Caroline, and Nick. I won a lot of plushies at claw machines. Mostly I remember being overwhelmed but s'all good. #skitrip2013

Big Bear ski trip = skiing for the first time~ There were nice people that helped me out with skiing. Cooking curry was interesting. Mark got me ice cream for my bday. :D People talking about personal things... This ski trip was shorter but people really took advantage of the time to bond. #skitrip2014

What's your favorite annual event and why? Culture Night, because I think working hard on it every year since I've joined has contributed to my desire to stick with CA. It's amazingly big~ I remember thinking CA Board is amazing for making it happen and I wanted to do that too.

Ski Trip is also very awesome, as it's the first big opportunity to have enough time to bond with people on a whole 'nother level.

What's your favorite part of Culture Night? Having everyone work together to put on the show! I might join skit again, since that was fun. But besides that there's a chance I might be put in charge of something if there are vacant positions in Culture Night Board.
