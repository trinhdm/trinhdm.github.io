About Chinese Association

Chinese Association is a social, cultural, and community service-based club located at the University of California, Irvine. Our mission is to share Chinese and Chinese-American culture with the greater Orange County and Los Angeles area.

As an entirely student-run organization, we host a variety of weekly club meetings and events, as well as multiple largescale annual events throughout the year. We welcome everyone to join us! Make life-long friends, contribute to the community, and participate in sharing the history and art of Chinese and Chinese-American culture. See you at our next meeting!

Membership

Chinese Association is always open to new members! Whether you are a college freshman, a recent graduate, or anywhere in between, you are welcome to join us at our next meeting. Find your big, meet your family, and get to know your fellow members and board officers!

During the school year, meetings are every Thursday @ 7pm at the Ring Room in the Cross Cultural Center.

  • learn more

Official membership is only $30 for the entire year and includes:

  • This year's newly designed CAUCI t-shirt
  • Exclusive discounts to select restaurants and businesses across Orange and Los Angeles county
  • Discounts to select after-events and social mixers
  • Opportunities to join our sports, hip hop dance, martial arts, and Culture Night performance teams
  • Opportunities to run for a board position for the 2016-2017 school year

While all members can join our events, only official members have access to the above benefits. Curious? Come out to our next meeting! For questions regarding membership, please email secretary@cauci.com.
