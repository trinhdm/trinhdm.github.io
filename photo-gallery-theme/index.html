Remember my name: A Portfolio Theme for Photographers

Hi! My name is Doreen Trinh and I'm a junior web developer. This theme was inspired by a very good friend of mine who mentioned that he wanted to get into photography. The theme's title hailed from the band/song that I was listening to while I was typing out this nifty about section (Yuna - Remember My Name).

As a photographer, your portfolio is your business card. Having an online presence to showcase your work is crucial as this world becomes increasingly digitized. As your online portfolio may be one of the first things you show to potential clients, your website's design is as important as the contents of your portfolio.

While wireframing and designing this theme, I wanted it be simple. I didn't want words to swarm the site; rather, I wanted the photos to be the first thing that captured the user's eyes. I thought a grid of photos with hover-over captions perfectly captured the crucial elements that make up a great photography portfolio website design.
